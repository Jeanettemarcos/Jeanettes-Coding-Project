The Life of Kanye West

Kanye Omari West is an American rapper, songwriter, record producer, fashion designer, and entrepreneur. West is 39 years old and he was born on June 8, 1977 in Atlanta, Georgia. He was raised in Chicago Illinois. His wife is Kim Kardashian and he has two kids North and Saint West. Kanye is well known for his Grammy-winning music, successful fashion line and controversial antics. He left college to pursue a musical career, producing tracks for Jay-Z while polishing his demo. He released The College Dropout in 2004. It sold 2.6 million copies and won Best Rap Album. His follow-up releases have been equally successful and West has become a celebrity famous for his outrageous and entertaining antics and marriage to reality television star Kim Kardashian. Kanye released many albums. In 2016, West released his seventh studio album The Life of Pablo, which debuted at number one on the Billboard 200, and began the Saint Pablo Tour to support the album in August of that year. Kanye made a name for himself in fashion. He debuted a line of shoes he designed for Louis Vuitton and also in Adidas made Yeezy Boots and his Yeezy Season clothing line.

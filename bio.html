This Life of Kendall Jenner

Kendall Nicole Jenner is an American fashion model and television personality. Originally known for appearing in the E! reality television show Keeping Up with the Kardashians, Jenner is now also a model in a movement dubbed "the Instagirl era" by Vogue magazine, or "Social Media Modeling." Kendall was born on November 3, 1955. She is currently at the age of 21. Her parents are Kris and Bruce Jenner. She has one sister: Kylie Jenner. She has six half siblings: Kourtney Kardashian, Kim Kardashian, Brandon Jenner, Brody Jenner, Khloé Kardashian, and Rob Kardashian. She walked the runways for high fashion designers during the New York, Milan, and Paris Fashion Weeks. Kendall Jenner did multiple editorials and cover shoots for LOVE and various international Vogue editions, walked for Victoria's Secret, and acts as brand ambassador for Estée Lauder's multimedia ad campaigns.
